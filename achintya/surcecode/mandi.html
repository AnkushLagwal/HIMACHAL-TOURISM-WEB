MANDI:


Famous Places Around Mandi :

1. Pandoh Dam:
13 kms from city center 1 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi At a height of 1360 m and nearly 16 kms from Mandi, Pandoh is a slender lake with crystal clear water. The picturesque surrounding of the lake is extremely captivating, serene and calm. It makes sure you leave this place completely refreshed.


2. Rewalsar Lake:
12 kms from city center 2 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi Rewalsar Lake is a spot of high spiritual importance. Situated 25 kms from Mandi, this place is well known for its seven floating islands. It is believed that an act of penance was held by Lomas Rishi here and Lord Shiva impressed by his devotion gave him seven floating islands as reward.


3. Parashar Lake:
17 kms from city center 3 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi At a distance of 40 kms from Mandi, this lake is a perfect site for experiencing pure nature and spiritual bliss. Surrounded by lush green land, verdant hills and clear gray water of the lake, one can witness a pagoda style three tiered temple by the lake.


4. Janjehli:
44 kms from city center 4 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi A treat for adventure buffs, Janjehli is an ideal place for activities like trekking. With a trail up to 3300 m, this place is almost 67 kms from Mandi.


5. Sunder Nagar:
19 kms from city center 5 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi With stunning landscape and a gorgeous fertile valley, Sunder Nagar is situated on the National Highway. A perfect place to take a break, the place is known for Asia's biggest Hydel Project with the confluence of two major rivers, Sutlej and Beas.


6. Trekking:
8,772 kms from city center 6 out of 17 Places To Visit in Mandi It's lush green valleys, interspersed with rivers and lakes offer one of the most picturesque and enjoyable trekking experiences.
