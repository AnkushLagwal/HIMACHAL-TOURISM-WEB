    Best Places for Camping In Himachal Pradesh



    1. Beas Kund

    It is one of the most popular treks from Manali and the environment and scenery it offers are one of the unique and delightful eye views for all the campers and trekkers. One of the best things about this trek is within a short period, which is perfect for Beginners too. It is 570 km from Delhi, and the nearest airport is in Bhuntar which is at a distance of around 50 km. Many campsites are available in this way, but it is best to carry your camping tents for a hassle-free trip.



    2. Triund

    It is a small hill station in Himachal Pradesh which is in the foot Dhauladhar ranges. Triund has a vast land of green grass and one of the accessible campsites among campers and trekkers and the evening sky from Triund is an excellent view to pitch a tent for camping. The rejuvenating atmosphere of the camp is its specialty which makes people from all over the world come for trekking and camping in Himachal.



    3. Tosh

    It is a small town near Kasol with beautiful weather, waterfalls and streams of soothing music for all the visitors. It is one of the popular locations of all the trekkers because of its location near Kasol. It is a 22km ride from Kasol and buses are easily available 24 hours of the day which makes traveling hassle-free.



    4. Chalal

    It is a small trekking and camping site near Kasol which is connected by a narrow cable bridge to Kasol. It is a destination which is mostly preferred by nature-lovers. The place features tall pines and happening cafes which makes camping in Himachal sound perfect.



    5. Rasol

    If you want to escape the crowd in Kasol, this place is the best fit for you. Situated in a charming village in the Parvati valley. The place offers rigorous treks, steep mountains, and Temple of Jamdagni Rishi is also located here. The greenery around and the calmness will soothe your senses completely.



    6. Malana

    Malana village is located in the mountaintop of the Himalayas’ making the uphill trek difficult through the difficult terrains. Only a few kilometers away from Kasol, this campsite is famous among many trekkers and campers and this is one of the best places for riverside camping in Himachal.



    7. Buddhaban

    After Tosh, this is the place where most of the trekkers camp as it offers beautiful scenery and excellent activities for travelers to spend their leisure time. It is just a short trek from Tosh Village near Kasol. Government buses and private taxis are running round the clock for all those who do not wish to go walking to the campsites.

    8. Grahan

    It is one of the most visited places for trekkers from New Delhi and Chandigarh. 10 km far from Kasol, it is a place where people visit to experience nature’s tranquillity. Located in Parvati Valley, Grahan has a scenic campsite and welcoming locals. The trek is a walk across the forest and consumption of alcohol makes it one of the best sites for camping in Himachal with families.



    9. Kutla Glacier

    Surrounded by breathtaking greenery and at a comfortable walking distance of 8 km from Tosh, Kutla Glacier, gives an excellent sights of the mountains, a beautiful village and some forest stretches along the trek.



    10. Rudranag

    With majestic waterfalls name Rudranag falls which lie in the route of Kheerganga, the trails of Rudranag cut through the rocky terrains and highly uneven paths which makes it highly challenging treks for the trekkers. The most enchanting attraction near kasol is Rudranag because of its sharp and steep falls.



    11. Kheerganga

    It is one the most daunting treks in the Himalayas region and India, but still it attracts lots of trekkers and campers because of the myth that it is a place where Lord Shiva meditated for thousands of years. This trek is covered with dense pines and numerous streams which gives a soothing, refreshing time to all its trekkers and is one of the best places for camping in Himachal.
